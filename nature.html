Fibonacci Patterns in the Living World

Sunflower Seed Arrangement

Sunflower seeds grow in spirals that follow Fibonacci numbers, allowing maximum packing efficiency and optimal exposure to sunlight.

Pine Cone Spirals

Pine cones exhibit clockwise and counter-clockwise spirals, often matching consecutive Fibonacci numbers like 8 and 13.

Leaf Arrangement (Phyllotaxis)

Leaves grow at Fibonacci angles to minimize overlap, ensuring each leaf receives maximum sunlight for photosynthesis.
